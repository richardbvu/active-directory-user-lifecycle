# RDP Admin Login Troubleshooting

## Purpose
Use this runbook when an administrator account cannot sign in to a workstation through Remote Desktop.

## Scope
Windows domain-joined workstation in the lab environment.

## Symptoms
- Administrator account cannot log in through RDP
- RDP may be enabled, but admin access still fails
- Standard troubleshooting suggests the account should already have access

## Checks
1. Confirm the target workstation is powered on and reachable
2. Confirm Remote Desktop is enabled on the target workstation
3. Confirm the administrator account is enabled and not locked out
4. Confirm the account is actually a member of a group that gives admin rights on the target machine
5. Confirm the account is effectively included in local `Administrators` or local `Remote Desktop Users`
6. Confirm the account is allowed by policy to log on through Remote Desktop Services
7. Confirm there is no policy or local setting denying remote logon
8. After changes, sign out and test again with a fresh logon
9. Run policy refresh if needed and retest
