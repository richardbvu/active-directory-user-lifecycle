Ticket: GPO appeared not to apply when validating policy results on workstation

Impact:
This created uncertainty about whether the workstation baseline policy was being enforced correctly on the domain-joined client.

Symptoms:

* Baseline workstation GPO appeared not to apply when running "gpresult /r".
* No policies visible under **User Settings**.
* Workstation configuration changes expected from GPO were not immediately confirmed.

Troubleshooting:

* Verified workstation computer object location in Active Directory.
* Confirmed baseline GPO was linked to **Workstations OU**.
* Ran `gpupdate /force` on workstation.
* Ran `gpresult /r` to review applied policies.
* Reviewed **User Settings** section initially and observed no applied GPOs.
* Rechecked results under **Computer Settings**.
* Confirmed workstation baseline GPO listed under computer policy scope.

Resolution:
GPO was correctly applied to the workstation computer object.
Initial validation focused on **User Settings**, while the policy was a **Computer Configuration policy linked to the Workstations OU**.
Policy application confirmed under **Computer Settings** using `gpresult`.
