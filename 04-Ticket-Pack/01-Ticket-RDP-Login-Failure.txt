Ticket: RDP login failure after moving workstation computer to Corp1 OU.

Impact:
Remote administrative access to the workstation was temporarily unavailable, preventing RDP-based management until the correct policy was applied and credentials refreshed.

Symptoms:
- RDP connection denied.
- Local "Remote Desktop Users" group initially empty.

Troubleshooting:
- Verified Remote Desktop enabled.
- Checked local group membership.
- Added CORP/Domain Admins to Remote Desktop Users under Computer Management\Local Users and Groups\Groups
- Verified GPO scope and OU placement.
- Linked Corp1-Workstation-RDP policy to Corp1 OU.
- Forced policy refresh.
- Refresh credentials


Resolution:
RDP login successful after policy applied and group membership validated and refresh credentials
