I was validating a workstation Group Policy in my Active Directory lab and it initially looked like the policy wasn’t applying when I ran gpresult /r. I checked the OU placement, confirmed the GPO link, and forced a policy refresh with gpupdate /force. After reviewing the results more carefully, I realized I was looking at the User Settings section, but the policy was a Computer Configuration GPO linked to the Workstations OU. When I checked the Computer Settings section using gpresult /scope computer /r, the policy was applied correctly. The issue wasn’t the GPO, I was simply validating the wrong section of the results.
