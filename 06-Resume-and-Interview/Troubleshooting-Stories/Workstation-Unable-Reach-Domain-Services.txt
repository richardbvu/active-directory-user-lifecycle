Workstation Unable to Reach Domain Services Due to Incorrect DNS

While testing workstation connectivity to domain services in my lab, I changed the client’s DNS configuration to simulate a network issue.

After the change, the workstation could no longer resolve the corp.test domain. I verified the network configuration with ipconfig /all and confirmed the DNS server was incorrect. I then used nslookup corp.test, which confirmed that domain name resolution was failing.

After correcting the DNS setting to point back to the domain controller, name resolution worked again and the workstation could reach domain services.
